- **Who buys:** Urban planners, construction companies
- **What they get:** Geographic distribution of infrastructure issues
- **Use case:** Prioritize road repairs, plan maintenance budgets
- **Price:** ‚Ç¨500/month per city

#### 2. **Trend Analysis Reports**
- **Who buys:** Policy researchers, NGOs
- **What they get:** Monthly/quarterly reports on issue trends
- **Use case:** Policy recommendations, grant applications
- **Price:** ‚Ç¨300/month

#### 3. **Predictive Maintenance Data**
- **Who buys:** Utility companies, municipalities
- **What they get:** AI-predicted infrastructure failures
- **Use case:** Proactive maintenance, cost savings
- **Price:** ‚Ç¨1,200/month

- **Who buys:** Political campaigns, government agencies
- **What they get:** Aggregated sentiment on civic issues

- Municipality approval required for DaaS in their city

**Revenue Share:**
- Municipality gets 20% of DaaS revenue from their city's data
- Incentivizes municipalities to promote citizen engagement

## üí° Key Success Factors

### 1. **Prove Value Early**
- Free pilot with Tripoli
- Measure and document ROI
- Get testimonials from officials and citizens

### 2. **Focus on Municipality Sales First**
- Most predictable revenue
- Validates the platform
- Enables other revenue streams

### 3. **Build DaaS Products Gradually**
- Start with simple dashboards
- Validate demand before building complex products
- Ensure privacy compliance

### 4. **Premium Features = Low Effort, High Margin**
- Mostly UI changes (remove ads, add filters)
- Automated billing (Stripe)
- Scales infinitely

### 5. **Consulting = Cash Flow Bridge**
- Helps cover costs while building SaaS base
- Builds relationships with municipalities
- Leads to upsells and referrals
